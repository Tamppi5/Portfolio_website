About Me

I'm an Engineering Psychology student from Aalto University. For a long time, I've been interested in psychology, math and computer science. I luckily found a way to combine these interests by studying how game-theoretical models can be applied for human-computer interaction. Currently, I aim to apply these skills to create solutions that make new technologies easier to work with and adopt. In my free time I like to sing in a choir, play piano and do various sports. On this website you can find projects I have worked on. Hope you enjoy my portfolio! :)
